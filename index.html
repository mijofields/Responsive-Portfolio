About Me

I was born within the sound of Bow Bells, on the last Square Root Day of the 20th Century. It was recorded that at the very moment of my birth a Cockerel crowed and lightening struck, somehere.

I am a collector of comics, perusor of literature (English and Spanish), rider of bikes, cruiser of longboards, manager of risk, traveller of the world, seeker of information, reciter of jokes, devourer of tacos, cooker of feasts, supporter of Spurs, lover of cats, congealer of ice cream, consumer of gin and ruminator of everything.
